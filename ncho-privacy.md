# Privacy Policy for Ncho Game

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2024

**Effective Date:** December 5, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Ncho Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children who use our game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile/desktop application.

**Age Requirement:** Ncho Game is suitable for players aged 5 years and above. We take special care to protect the privacy of children in accordance with applicable laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

Ncho Game is designed with privacy in mind. We **DO NOT** collect:

- ❌ Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos, videos, or camera access
- ❌ Contacts or address book information
- ❌ Social media information
- ❌ Payment or credit card information (the game is free)
- ❌ Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- ❌ Behavioral or usage analytics across other apps
- ❌ Any information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction)

### 2.2 Information We Store Locally

The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers:

✅ **Game Settings:**
- Difficulty level preference (Easy/Medium/Hard)
- Audio volume preferences (Music and Sound Effects)
- Game state and progress

✅ **Game Statistics:**
- Rounds won/lost/drawn
- Games played
- High scores and achievements

**Note:** This data is stored using Unity's PlayerPrefs system and remains on your device. We cannot access this information.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no use of personal data. The locally stored game data is used solely to:

- Save your game preferences
- Maintain your game progress
- Provide a personalized gaming experience
- Track achievements and statistics offline

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage Only

All game data is stored locally on your device using:
- Unity PlayerPrefs (for settings and preferences)
- Local device storage (for game states)

### 4.2 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect locally stored data:
- Data is stored in encrypted format when possible
- No transmission of data over the internet
- No third-party access to your game data

### 4.3 Data Retention

Game data remains on your device until you:
- Uninstall the application
- Clear the app data/cache
- Reset settings within the game
